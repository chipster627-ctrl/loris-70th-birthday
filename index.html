Charleston Charms ‚ú®

Fun facts about the Holy City!

üèõÔ∏è Historic City

Charleston was founded in 1670, making it one of America's oldest cities with over 350 years of history!

üçç Symbol of Hospitality

The pineapple is Charleston's symbol of welcome! Look for them on gates, fountains, and door knockers.

üåà Rainbow Row

The colorful houses on Rainbow Row were painted in the 1930s to brighten up the city during tough times.

‚õ™ The Holy City

Charleston earned this nickname for its 400+ churches and long history of religious tolerance.

ü¶ê Culinary Heritage

Charleston is the birthplace of she-crab soup and shrimp & grits - lowcountry classics we'll definitely taste!

üé¨ Hollywood South

Many movies and TV shows have been filmed here, including The Notebook, Dear John, and Southern Charm!
